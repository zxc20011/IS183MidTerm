Gourmet Coffee

Have you tried the new Coffee called "Coffee Maximum"? The coffee that was awarded as the Coffee Of The Year by Zhihong? If not! This is the site where you will get all the information you need before trying out the newest version of "Coffee Maximum"!

Basic Information : The Gourmet Coffee was created in 1999 by our genius coffee brewer, Bryce Svolveyo. This coffee shop was based on the story of a dog named "Gourmet" who saved humanity from a war between Cocoa trees and Cocoa nuts. This dog was born from half Cocoa tree half Cocoa nut and travel to both heaven and hell to seek his past. Eventually, the dog realize the coffee was produced by shredding Cocoa nuts into coffee. He was determined to save Cocoa nuts from getting destroyed.

Main Ingredients :

Cocoa Nuts - 1000 Grams
